02/1989 - ?

Hello, my name is Clément Marion, I live in Brussels, and this is my MUSIC. I've been working with a lot of great artists in the past twenty years, and though I will definitely go on, I think it's time to also share some personal stuff.

My music is about finding sounds & harmonies put together in a way that makes me feel good. They could be beautiful, ugly, or boring. I’m constantly looking for sounds that resonate with home, like the the memory of the particular footsteps foley sounds in a movie I’ve see as a kid, stuff like this. Or “that one chord” that instantly makes my brother smile.

I’m not trying to be hyper modern nor going for a vintage referenced vibe.

So here it is, hopefully someone will connect with it :)
